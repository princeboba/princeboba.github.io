How to get a kitten?

Kitten • Feb 22, 2022• Cats

Thank you so much for considering getting a kitten from us. We are looking for loving and responsible parents that can take care of their kittens for life. As a result, we require each prospective parent to fill out an application.

Each pet quality kitten costs $2000-$2500, and each show quality kitten costs $2500-$3000. After your application is approved, we ask you to put down a $200 refundable deposit to secure your spot on the waitlist. You can transfer your deposit to a future litter, or get a refund after a year.

Each kitten will be dewormed, vaccinated according to their age, and neutered/spayed before they go home. The parentage of the kittens has been tested HCM negative by UC Davis.

We prefer parents to physical come and pick up their kittens, but shipping could be arranged at parents' expense. We don't provide international shipping.

Join the waitlist Step by Step:

1. Fill out an application
2. Pay a $200 refundable deposit

Our goal is to limit the wait time to under 6 months. If the waitlist is open, it generally means that we are expecting new litters.
