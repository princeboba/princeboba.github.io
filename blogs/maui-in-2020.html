Maui in 2020

Jeffrey Lin • September 7, 2020• Blogs

Hawaii has a 14 day required quarantine has been extended to at least October 1st: https://www.hawaiitourismauthority.org/covid-19-updates/
All travelers must undergo a 14 day quarantine: https://www.gohawaii.com/special-alerts-information.
If you are uncertain, please consider traveling here at another time; many airlines are offering ticket changes for other travel times. We do not know when the quarantine will be lifted; we are as in the dark as many of you regarding day-to-day and week-to-week changes to the travel restrictions.

Airfare

Southwest recently started flying to Hawaii from California, and the introductory rates are a great deal. I actually missed out on introductory rates of 199 round trip and booked for 19883 pts at 330 round trip. At 1.66 cents per point this is a pretty decent deal for Southwest pts which follow a fixed conversion of roughly 1.5-1.7 cpp. The other benefit of booking with points is the extreme flexibility as there are no rebooking fees and they are fully refundable even for wanna get away fares up to 10 minutes before departure time. This worked out as I checked back every week on the price and found that round trip tickets from sjc-ogg had dropped to 16000 points. I immediately rebooked online and was immediately credited my 3000+ points of savings. As an added bonus I hold the companion pass with Southwest earned through credit cards and was able to split the cost of airfare with a friend.

Maui Scuba Diving 5/5 stars

Can’t laze on the beach for a whole week right? Well actually, I probably can but might as well get some adventuring and physical activity in the mix as well.
We signed up to get our open water certifications at Maui Scuba Diving. We completed the PADI e learning ahead of time to save a day in the classroom.
Tip: The PADI e learning goes on sale at certain times (We managed to nab it 15% off on Black Friday!) (Also, if your employer offers health benefits such as FSA or wellness reimbursement the scuba might be a reimbursable expense!)

Wailea Beach - 4/5 stars

Of course every trip to Hawaii includes plenty of lazing on the beach, complete with amazing views. We stayed in an Airbnb in wailea, making it a short 5 min drive or 10 min walk to the beach.
Tips: The hotels along the beach all have public parking.
